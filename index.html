What Do You Know About Love?

Welcome!

At What Do You Know About Love? we celebrate the diversity and beauty of love in all its forms. Our platform invites individuals to share their personal experiences and perspectives on love through a simple Google Form. Participants select a question about love, provide their answers, and optionally include identifiers for context. We then format these responses into visually appealing response cards, enhanced with custom images. Explore the many facets of love shared by people from all walks of life and find inspiration in their wisdom and experiences.

What You Have Told Us about Love

What lessons have you learned from experiencing heartbreak or loss in love?

Heartbreak and loss have been tough for me. For a long time I felt like a disappointment and struggled to connect with others. Being rejected after dates left me feeling lost and questioning my worth. Over time, though, I've learned that it's okay to be myself and that I don't have to be right for everyone. I realized that the right person will appreciate me for who I am. Meeting David, who understands and accepts me, has taught me that past pain and loss were part of my journey towards self-acceptance and finding true love. Now I know that being myself is enough and that the right person will come along who values and understands me.

SJ She/Her Autistic Seattle, WA

What does love feel like?

To me, love feels like safety. It feels like being able to be completely vulnerable with your partner without judgement. It feels like a warm hug when you're really sad.

Savanna D. 25 Female Bisexual Tulsa, OK

What challenges have you faced in expressing love, and how did you overcome them?

As an autistic person, I do struggle to express love because in my head, I just assume you already know and don’t need to say it, but I remind myself all the time to make sure I’m showing that I still love and care for this person. If they hadn’t said anything I wouldn’t have made adjustments.

Ben C. He/Him 33 Bisexual Tulsa, OK
