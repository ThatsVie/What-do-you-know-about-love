What Do You Know About Love?

What do you know about love?

I’ve heard it said that grief is just love with no place to go, and I deeply believe this. My sister Patrisia died when we were teenagers, and for a long time, I coped with that immense grief by causing myself harm. I felt lost, overwhelmed by a love that had no direction. I now know that healing lies in continuing to cultivate love and giving it a place to go. After she died, I felt immense guilt because I had been performing CPR until the ambulance arrived, and it didn’t feel like enough. Now, I understand her legacy is not defined by that moment but by the love she shared and the lives she inspires. Thank you for helping me keep her memory alive and allowing her love to find new places to grow.

Vie P, 34, Blissfully Committed, Founder and Developer of What Do You Know About Love?

About Us

This project was born from a deep, abiding love for love. This affection fuels the spirit of our mission, embodying the belief that love, in all its forms, from the joy of friendships and the bond with pets to the profound connections with loved ones and passionate commitment to causes, is a powerful force that shapes our lives and communities. We are dedicated to exploring and celebrating these diverse expressions of love, believing that by doing so, we can foster a more compassionate and connected world.

Submissions and Participation

Our content is sourced directly from the heartfelt responses of individuals who have participated in our project through a carefully designed form. This form allows people to share their personal experiences and perspectives on love, ensuring a broad and inclusive range of voices that reflect the diversity of human experiences.

Progressive and
Inclusive

We strive to create a progressive space that celebrates the diversity of love. Whether it's romantic, familial, platonic, or self-love, each story adds a unique thread to the rich fabric of human connection. Our platform is dedicated to inclusivity and respect, welcoming a wide range of perspectives. We aim to foster a safe environment where everyone, regardless of their background, identity, or beliefs, feels valued and heard.

Imagery and
Visuals

To visually represent the beauty and diversity of love, we utilize images generated by DALL-E. These images capture the essence of the stories shared, helping to create a visually engaging and evocative experience for our audience.

Our Vision

At the heart of this project lies a profound desire to cultivate an understanding of love. We aspire to inspire, educate, and connect people by sharing the many ways in which love appears in our lives. Our commitment is to provide a space that is both spiritually enriching and professionally curated, where the power of love is explored, celebrated, and shared.

Our vision is to foster a more compassionate and connected world, where love in all its forms is acknowledged and valued. We believe that by exploring the true nature of love and its transformative power, we can inspire a broader understanding and appreciation of this fundamental human experience.

Together, we can create a more compassionate, connected, and loving world.

FAQ

How can I contact you?

You can email info@whatdoyouknowaboutlove.com for more information.

How can I submit my response to a question?

You can submit your response by visiting the Submit a Response page.

Can I submit multiple responses?

Yes! Feel free to fill out the form as many times as you'd like.

Can I remain anonymous?

Yes! You have the option to identify yourself in any way you prefer. Submissions without a provided name are labeled as "Anonymous."

Who can participate in this project?

Anyone who can access the Google Form! This project is open to all individuals who wish to share their personal experiences and perspectives on love. We welcome and encourage participation from people of all backgrounds and walks of life.

Can I update or remove my submission?

Yes, contact info@whatdoyouknowaboutlove.com if you would like your submission removed or updated at any time.

Are you on social media?

As of now we are only on Instagram and have one account. You can visit us at instagram.com/whatdoyouknowaboutlove.

Why do you use AI-generated art?

AI art is used for this project because it allows for quickly and affordably generating high-quality images that enhance the visual appeal of the content. Time and financial limitations make AI art an effective solution. AI art provides a diverse range of styles and can be customized to fit specific needs. As this project grows, the hope is to pay artists and contribute original art to support the creative community and add a personal touch.

What inspired you to start this project?

This project began as a school assignment where the specific task was to create a custom front end from scratch. Love is always on my mind, and I apply it to all I do, including my projects. Over the trimester, each assignment inspired the idea further. A Bootstrap design project led to a pug love-themed page, and a local storage and cookies project resulted in a love journal. These assignments made me think about the simplicity and complexity of love, the consequences of its absence, and the various forms it takes. A significant inspiration for this project was my sister Patrisia. She exemplified love through her deep care for others, her commitment to justice, her authenticity, and her willingness to be vulnerable. Her life and legacy remind me of love’s immense power and the importance of sharing it. I wanted to create a platform that could make the world a little better by creating an archive of love and inviting people from all walks of life to share their experiences. The power of love is immense, and this project aims to explore and celebrate that higher power.

What future developments are planned for this project?

There are several exciting plans for expanding the project in the future. These include expanding our presence on social media to reach a wider audience, launching a podcast to interview people about various love themes and share their stories, and collaborating with artists to create original content. Additionally, we aim to generate funds to support community initiatives, adopt a pug, and engage in collaborations with other projects and organizations to promote the message of love and connection.
