What Do You Know About Love?

What do you know about love?

I’ve heard it said that grief is love with nowhere to go, and I believe it deeply. My sister Patrisia died when we were teenagers, and for a long time, my way of coping with that immense grief was to cause myself harm. I felt lost, overwhelmed by a love that had no direction. Now, I understand that the answer lies in continuing to cultivate love and giving that love a place to go. Patrisia taught me so much about love: how to care deeply for those we cherish, stand up for the rights of others, be authentic, and embrace vulnerability. She experienced profound suffering, but she also knew immense joy. Patrisia was loved deeply by many and continues to be. When she passed away from asthma, we donated her organs. Through her donation, Patrisia continues to live on in every loving action and the lives she extended. Her love and spirit resonate through the lives she touched, extending far beyond her time with us. When she died, I felt immense guilt because I was rendering first aid until the ambulance arrived, and it didn’t feel like enough. Today, I understand that her legacy is not defined by that moment but by the love she gave and the lives she continues to inspire. Thank you for helping me keep her memory alive and for allowing her love to continue to find new places to grow.

Vie P, 34, Blissfully Committed, Founder and Developer of What Do You Know About Love?

About Us

This project was born from a deep, abiding love for love. This affection fuels the spirit of our mission, embodying the belief that love, in all its forms, from the joy of friendships and the bond with pets to the profound connections with loved ones and passionate commitment to causes, is a powerful force that shapes our lives and communities. We are dedicated to exploring and celebrating these diverse expressions of love, believing that by doing so, we can foster a more compassionate and connected world.

Submissions and Participation

Our content is sourced directly from the heartfelt responses of individuals who have participated in our project through a carefully designed form. This form allows people to share their personal experiences and perspectives on love, ensuring a broad and inclusive range of voices that reflect the diversity of human experiences.

Progressive and
Inclusive

We strive to create a progressive space that celebrates the diversity of love. Whether it's romantic, familial, platonic, or self-love, each story adds a unique thread to the rich fabric of human connection. Our platform is dedicated to inclusivity and respect, welcoming a wide range of perspectives. We aim to foster a safe environment where everyone, regardless of their background, identity, or beliefs, feels valued and heard.

Imagery and
Visuals

To visually represent the beauty and diversity of love, we utilize images generated by DALL-E. These images capture the essence of the stories shared, helping to create a visually engaging and evocative experience for our audience.

Our Vision

At the heart of this project lies a profound desire to cultivate an understanding of love. We aspire to inspire, educate, and connect people by sharing the many ways in which love appears in our lives. Our commitment is to provide a space that is both spiritually enriching and professionally curated, where the power of love is explored, celebrated, and shared.

Our vision is to foster a more compassionate and connected world, where love in all its forms is acknowledged and valued. We believe that by exploring the true nature of love and its transformative power, we can inspire a broader understanding and appreciation of this fundamental human experience.

Together, we can create a more compassionate, connected, and loving world.

Why AI Art?

AI art is used for this project because it allows for quickly and affordably generating high-quality images that enhance the visual appeal of the content. Time and financial limitations make AI art an effective solution. AI art provides a diverse range of styles and can be customized to fit specific needs. As this project grows, the hope is to pay artists and contribute original art to support the creative community and add a personal touch.

Why do you use Google Forms?

A Google Form is used for this project because it is reliable and efficient. While exploring other options, I faced challenges in securing the endpoints and database. The built-in security features of Google Forms protect data and prevent spam, ensuring the integrity of the responses. The form seamlessly integrates with Google Sheets for easy data storage and analysis. It is free to use with generous limits suitable for our current needs. The ease of use for participants was a significant consideration. Other options required users to authenticate their email or create a login, which deterred some participants. As the project scales and expands, other options will be considered to better accommodate increased requirements and potentially offer more features on the site.

Why do you include donation links?

The inclusion of Stripe and Venmo links is to welcome financial support, which helps cover the costs of maintaining and improving this project. As a student, such contributions are invaluable. Some users have expressed interest in supporting the project financially, so these links are provided for convenience. While your contributions are greatly appreciated, there is absolutely no obligation to contribute.

What inspired you to start this project?

This project began as a school assignment where the specific task was to create a custom front end from scratch. Love is always on my mind, and I apply it to all I do, including my projects. Over the trimester, each assignment inspired the idea further. A Bootstrap design project led to a pug love-themed page, and a local storage and cookies project resulted in a love journal. These assignments made me think about the simplicity and complexity of love, the consequences of its absence, and the various forms it takes. A huge inspiration for this project was my sister Patrisia, who taught me so much about love: caring for those we cherish, standing up for the rights of others, being authentic, and embracing vulnerability. Her life and legacy remind me of the immense power of love and the importance of sharing it. I wanted to create a platform that could make the world a little better by creating an archive of love and inviting people from all walks of life to share their experiences. The power of love is immense, and this project aims to explore and celebrate that higher power.

How can I support the project other than financially?

You can support this project by spreading the word. Post about it on social media, and tell your friends and family. Submit a response and share your own experiences about love. Practice self-love and show love to others in your life. You can also send pictures of your pug!

Do you have plans to expand the project in the future?

Yes, there are several exciting plans for expanding the project in the future. These include expanding our presence on social media to reach a wider audience, launching a podcast to interview people about various love themes and share their stories, and collaborating with artists to create original content. Additionally, we aim to generate funds to support community initiatives, adopt a pug, and engage in collaborations with other projects and organizations to promote the message of love and connection.
